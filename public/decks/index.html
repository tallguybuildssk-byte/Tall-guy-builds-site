Tall Guy Builds Inc. has been building custom decks for Regina homeowners for over 20 years. Whether you want a low-maintenance composite deck, a classic pressure-treated build, or a hardwood showpiece, we deliver quality craftsmanship on time and on budget.

Deck Materials We Work With

Composite decking (Trex, TimberTech, Fiberon) is our specialty — fade-resistant, splinter-free, and virtually maintenance-free. Pressure-treated lumber is a cost-effective choice for Regina's climate. We also work with cedar and hardwood for premium builds.

Foundations Built for Regina's Frost

Regina's freeze-thaw cycles can heave a poorly-built deck in a single winter. We use helical screw piles or properly set concrete footings drilled below the frost line, ensuring your deck stays level for decades.

Railing, Lighting & Custom Features

We offer aluminum, glass, cable, and wood railing systems. Ask about built-in bench seating, planters, pergolas, and low-voltage deck lighting to complete your outdoor space.
