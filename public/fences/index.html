Fence Contractor in Regina, SK

Tall Guy Builds Inc. installs privacy fences, picket fences, and custom gates for homeowners across Regina, White City, and Pilot Butte. We build fences that look great and last through Saskatchewan's harshest winters.

Fence Materials

Cedar is the premium choice — naturally rot-resistant and beautiful. Pressure-treated pine offers excellent durability at a lower cost. We help you choose the right material for your property and budget.

Built for Regina's Wind

Regina is one of Canada's windiest cities. Our fences are built to handle it — properly spaced posts, correct post depth, and appropriate panel construction that resists wind load without racking or blowing over.

Privacy Fences & Gates

We build 6-foot and 8-foot privacy fences with a range of styles. Single and double gates, decorative post caps, lattice tops, and colour staining are all available options.

Permits & Property Lines

Fence permits are required in Regina for fences over a certain height or on corner lots. We handle permit applications and help you understand setback requirements before we break ground.
